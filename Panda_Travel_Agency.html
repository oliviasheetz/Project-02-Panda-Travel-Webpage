Hot Water Beach in New Zealand!

More Information About Hot Water Beach

What this location has to offer:

Hot Water Beach is an area located on Mercury Bay on the East Coast of New Zealand. The naturally heated water comes from deep within the sand, causing the water to heat quickly. If you dig deep enough, you can create your own hot water spa to lay in. The waves make this beach perfect for surfers as well! There are also many other fun and exciting activities for all kind of people. Some of these activities include:

  • zip lining
  • skydiving
  • rafting
  • sailing
  • diving
  • windsurfing & kitesurfing

Other things to do around Hot Water Beach:

If you are looking for an escape from the hot, sunny beach, you can take a trip to the near by city of Auckland. Hot Water Beach is located about 2 hours, by car, from Auckland, New Zealand. Auckland it is one of the major cities in New Zealand. The Sky Tower, a famous land mark in the city, has great views of Viaduct Harbour. This is just a quick drive away from the beautiful beach.
